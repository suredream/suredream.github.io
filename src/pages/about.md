# About Me

Jun Xiong, an AI/ML engineer with a background in geospatial intelligence, production ML systems in San Francisco area.

This blog has no interests to produce more AI slop, but a place for me to ruminate on my own past thoughts, experiences, and unfinished questions, so that I may become more fluent when facing real problems, turn lived experience and hard-won observations into reusable models.

The raw material came from my own writing / notes / memo, I only use AI for polishing or translating. Part of the reason I keep writing here is to preserve my own sense of language: to remain someone who forms sentences, not merely someone who consumes them.

If you enjoy what you read here and would like to support more writing like this, you can [buy me a coffee](https://ko-fi.com/suredream).
