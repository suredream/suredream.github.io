# About

I am an AI/ML engineer with a background in geospatial intelligence, remote sensing, production ML systems, and AI-native workflows.

This blog is a public archive of working models: problem definition, work capability, AI-assisted knowledge assets, ML systems, and judgment in the AI age.

The goal is not to publish high-frequency commentary. The goal is to turn lived experience and hard-won observations into reusable models.

## What I Write About

- AI-assisted work and knowledge assets
- Problem definition and scope control
- Work capability and career strategy
- ML systems and production validation
- Judgment, attention, and long-term learning
